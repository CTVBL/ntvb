Schedule

The season is to begin Oct 01, 2002.

Games start at 6:45 pm and must be finished by 9:45 pm. There will be three to four teams at each gym - teams that are not playing are responsible for refereeing, keeping lines, and scoring.  Game formats and score sheets can be downloaded here (SCORE SHEETS) for Recreational, Intermediate (3 team and 4 team formats) and Competitive divisions.

Week 1 games on Oct 01 will be exhibition only.
