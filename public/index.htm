WELCOME TO CENTRAL TORONTO VOLLEYBALL  

CENTRAL TORONTO VOLLEYBALL is an indoor co-ed volleyball league for adults. The league is based in Toronto Ontario. Games are held Tuesday evenings from 6:45pm to 9:30pm at local public schools around Bayview & Eglinton, Bayview & Sheppard, and Lawrence & Avenue.
