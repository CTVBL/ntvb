WELCOME TO CENTRAL TORONTO VOLLEYBALL  

CENTRAL TORONTO VOLLEYBALL is an indoor co-ed volleyball league for adults. The league is based in Toronto Ontario. Games are held Tuesday evenings from 6:45pm to 9:30pm at local public schools around Bayview & Eglinton, Bayview & Sheppard, and Lawrence & Avenue. The 2018/2019 season will start on October 2, 2018 and will run until April 30, 2019.

2018/19 SEASON REGISTRATION OPEN
