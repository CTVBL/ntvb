SCHEDULE AND STANDINGS

A LADDER SYSTEM will be used for the ranking of teams and week to week scheduling.. Winners move up a gym, losers move down a gym, and middle placed teams stay put. At the top school, the top two teams stay put. At the bottom school, the bottom two teams stay put.  Middle placed teams are asked to take the league equipment, as they will return to the same school the next week.

All team captains are to report results by end of day Thursday (rank teams first, second, and third). Email to contact@centraltorontovolleyball.com. Teams that do not report scores by this deadline may be ranked third and may move down.

Permits at all schools are from 6:45 PM to 9:45 PM. Games are to start at 7:00 PM and end at 9:30 PM sharp.
